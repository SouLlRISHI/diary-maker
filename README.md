Step 1: Diary Basic Details (Intro Spread)
Diary Title: Give your story a beautiful name (e.g., "Our Journey", "Love Story 2026").
Date: Enter a special date.
Story (Left Page): Write your long story or message here.
Poem/Message (Right Page): A short poem or a closing message.
Small Note: A tiny note for the corner of the page (e.g., "Forever and Always").
Step 2: Add Memories
You can add multiple memories.
For each memory:
Title: Name of the moment (e.g., "First Meeting").
Date: When did it happen?
Message: Describe this beautiful moment.
Photo Link: Paste the URL of any image.
Small Note: For any extra details.
Step 3: Preview
After filling everything, click the Preview button.
The diary will open:
Back Navigation: Click the left side to flip back.
Next Page: Click the right side to flip forward.
Audio: Background music starts automatically from page 2.
Step 4: Generate Link
Once satisfied, go back to the Maker and click Finish & Link.
You will receive a unique link.
